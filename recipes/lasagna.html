Simple homemade lasagna for the entire family to enjoy!

Makes an entire pan of creamy, cheesy lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozerella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degress
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Brown and crumble the beef for approximately 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
  3. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for about 5 minutes
  4. In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the grated parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread about 3/4 of the spaghetti sauce in a 9x13 baking dish.
  6. Cover the sauce with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese, 1/4 cup sauce. Repeate for another layer
  7. Add the final layer with 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese
  8. Pour 1/2 cup of water along the edges of the dish and cover tightly with aluminum foil
  9. Bake covered for 45 mins, then uncovered for another 10 minutes
  10. Take the lasagna out of the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
