Simple homemade burger recipe for a delicious meal!

Makes 4 juicy burgers.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pount ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat and lightly oil outdoor grill grate (high heat)
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl
  3. Add ground beef and breadcrumbs, mixing with a fork until well blended
  4. Separate into four 3/4 inch thick patties
  5. Cook covered for 6 to 8 minutes per side, ensuring the internal temperature reaches 160 degrees F
  6. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings
