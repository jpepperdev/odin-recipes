Enjoy a fresh and tasty taco salad that's perfect for any meal!

Makes 4 servings of delicious taco salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (16 oz) can chili beans
  • 1 (16 oz) bottle French dressing to taste
  • 1 (1.25 oz) pacakge taco seasoning mix
  • 1 (14.5 oz) package tortilla chips
  • 1 head shredded iceburg lettuce
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup prepared salsa
  • 4 tbsp sour cream

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown the ground beef until fully cooked and crumbly (approx 5 to 7 minutes). Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in the chili beans, French dressing, and taco seasoning mix.
  3. Fill the empty dressing bottle 2/3 full of water and add to the skilled. Simmer for about 15 minutes until heated through.
  4. Crush the bag of tortilla chips and place in a large serving bowl.
  5. Layer the shredded lettuce, beef mixture, cheddar cheese, tomatoes, salsa, and sour cream on top of the chips.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your taco salad!
